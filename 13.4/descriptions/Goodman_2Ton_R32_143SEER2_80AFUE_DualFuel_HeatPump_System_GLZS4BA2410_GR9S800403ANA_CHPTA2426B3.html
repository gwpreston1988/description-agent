GOODMAN 2 Ton 14.3 SEER2 R-32 80% AFUE Dual Fuel Heat Pump System (Horizontal Coil)

Experience ultimate year-round comfort with this complete Goodman 2-ton dual fuel heat pump system (Horizontal Coil). This intelligently matched system combines a high-efficiency 14.3 SEER2 / 7.5 HSPF2 heat pump for efficient heating and cooling, an 80% AFUE single-stage gas furnace with variable-speed ECM blower for supplemental heating during extreme cold, and a precision-engineered horizontal evaporator coil with factory-installed TXV. The system automatically switches between electric heat pump operation and gas furnace backup for optimal efficiency and comfort. Designed for dependable performance with environmentally-friendly R-32 refrigerant.
