GOODMAN 4 Ton Variable Capacity 17.5 SEER2 R-32 96% AFUE Dual Fuel System with ComfortNet Smart Thermostat (Horizontal Coil)

Experience ultimate year-round comfort with this complete Goodman 4-ton dual fuel HVAC system (Horizontal Coil). This advanced matched system combines a high-efficiency variable capacity 17.5 SEER2 heat pump, a 96% AFUE variable speed gas furnace with variable speed ECM blower, and a precision-engineered horizontal evaporator coil with factory-installed EEV. Featuring the GTST-CW-WH-A smart WiFi thermostat with ComfortNet communicating controls, this system offers seamless integration with voice assistants and mobile app control for ultimate convenience. The dual fuel configuration automatically switches between electric heat pump and gas furnace operation to optimize efficiency and comfort. Designed for exceptional year-round heating and cooling performance, this system delivers outstanding energy efficiency, quiet operation, and proven reliability with environmentally-friendly R-32 refrigerant.
