Solace 2 Ton R-32 Air Handler - S-AWST24SU1310A

The Solace S-AWST24SU1310A is a high-efficiency multi-position air handler designed for use with R-32 refrigerant systems. Featuring an advanced Variable Speed ECM blower motor, this unit delivers precise airflow control and reduced energy consumption.
